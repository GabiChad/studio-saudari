studio saudari

studio saudari is all about collaboration.

by showcasing the words + imagery of various artists, writers, academics and filmmakers in print magazines and books, we hope to highlight works which fuel critical commentary and present different perspectives.

born in berlin, residing in antwerp


Limited to 150 copies, Fruta explores various interpretations of the ultimate symbol - fruits. A navigation of conflicting themes - sustenance, fertility, violence, family, desire - fruits are woven into the personal, political, cultural.
